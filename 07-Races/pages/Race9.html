Mountaineer

As the name suggests, Mountaineers are acclimated to the high reaches of mountains, cliffsides, and even canyons. These tougher, more rugged conditions have shaped them into the hardy and robust people they are today. As a result of this, they have even developed their own coded calls, capable of traveling great distances for ease of communication to those farther than a typical voice can reach using the echo of their elevated environments. Their approach to life is one of ceaseless persistence. The bite of the climbs has not quelled the fire of determination within their hearts.
