Vocqua

- Infamous hunters and warriors of the sea, Vocqua have all but perfected the art of the trident. A unique weapon that serves not only as its intended function but as an extension of themselves. Unlike their kindly neighboring race, the Piscine, Vocqua prefer the seas to the sands. Only being able to last out of water for a year's time before needing to return to the crashing waves. Once in their element, they demonstrate just how in tune with their environment they really are. Jetting through water at speeds that would make most other aquatic life green with envy.

The race called Voca Aqua is routinely called Vocqua. They have under water communities called WaterTowns and WaterVillages. However, they are able to breath air and adjust to surface living for quite a while. Only after a year or so some issue may pop up. The resolution is a dip into water for a while and they are good.

Honorifics placed before FIRST names:

  • Shen:
    Cold water dwelling family line.

  • Alh:
    Archaic form of ‘Gul‘ which is still used by many families of that once had power and still honor that history.

  • Gul:
    Generational notation of service to the community.

  • Lish:
    Indication of a family known for money and power.

  • Alsh:
    Families from the elder race that once could survive under the pressures of the deep oceans.
