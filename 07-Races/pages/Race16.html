Piscine-Fey

The Piscine, or fin folk, as they often refer to themselves, are an enchanting subaquatic race with both fish and elven-like features. Primarily, they reside in temperate underwater cities or coastal villages, towns, and are commonly neighbors with the Vocqua. Despite being known as a water-dwelling race, they are well equipped to live above the surface thanks to a unique adaptation that, when exposed to open air, their webbing retracts and skin dries.

Piscine have few honorifics with little pomp and circomstance to note the lineage.
