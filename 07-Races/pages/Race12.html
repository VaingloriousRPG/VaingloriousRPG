Presma

Still furry from their feline ancestry, Presma are well adapted to their individual environment of origin. Being found in areas as differing as tropics to tundras, there is not much the cunning Presma wouldn’t try to overcome and adapt to. These agile bipedal cats also feature a heightened sense of hearing and vision to boot, making them an excellent employ for hunting or rough work.

Presma society evolved as a matriarchy intertwined with that of the Dark Elves in a time where the latter held sway over mankind. As times evolved from the harsh past of Dark Elves the men slowly gained more prominence which has resulted in more men in positions of trust and power. Notwithstanding, the majority of those positions are still held by women.

Honorifics placed before Last names:

  • Rahn:
    Oldest of the bloodlines. Some will have the same last name but without the ‘Rahn’ which indicates a joining from another family into this line of the oldest.

  • Mawl:
    Affectation within some families indicating a close knit families.

  • Phren:
    Family line comes from a snow and ice area. Arctic lands.

  • Knuel:
    This clearly states a connection to royalty. If the family lacks the connection even within one generation this is removed. This is very very easy to verify when within the boundaries of the sovereign.
