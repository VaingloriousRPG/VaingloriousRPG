Murik

Descendent from a handful of dwarves who felt there was much more to the world than a subterranean lifestyle, the Murik now make themselves right at home on the surface. Over the generations, the Murik’s eyesight overcame the glares of the sun, and their low-light vision shifted to ultraviolet. The remnants of an existence in the darkness below pathed the way towards a life in a new sort of shade, a night under the stars. Unlike their root-kin, they’ve got no issues with socializing and do well in fields of commerce. No matter the face they greet, they are more than happy to do business.

No cultural titles of honor. Titles are given within the context of the game as needed. These surface dwelling Dwarves rarely have obvious indications of bloodlines within their names. But often a middle name is given.
