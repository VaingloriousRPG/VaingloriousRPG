Sylvan

Battle Role:

Nature Casters

  • Stays back, has some healing and some damaging spells. May make Nae'Ems with Hunter partners.
  • Attacks: Ice Spells and Small hand weapons.
  • Armor: Clothing and Leather (no metal/shields)
  • Avoids combat, has few hit points.
  • Specialties: Interact with Flora and Fauna, Assume Animal Forms, Weather and Water/Ice.
  • Focus Item: Wand
  • Throw spells to create amalgam animals of natural medium, get water and food, and connects with hunters via Nae'Em.

Primary attributes:

  • Acumen
