Fighter

These melee specialists bear the brunt of enemy attacks. Racial traits and ability adjustments can determine a fighters style. Wielding a fearsome duo-handed sword or bracing against an onslaught in field-plate and shield, racial choice will enhance or compliment your fighting style.

Fighters create a barrier between the enemy and the fighter's less armored companions. Unlike soldiers in a military formation, adventurer fighters must excel at controlling their immediate vicinity. Though others may protect the flanks, a few fighters can hold the line.

Battle Role:

Holds the line

  • Generally, only 1-3 in a party, including NPCs. Generally, coordinates but does not set formations.
  • Use charge to close and engage enemy fighter types in melee.
  • Attacks: Melee and some ranged/thrown.
  • Armor: All types of armor and shields.
  • Specializes in close-in fighting using melee weapons such as a sword or mace.
  • Specialties: Battle, Lift/Bend, Intimidation, Leadership.
