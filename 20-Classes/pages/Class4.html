Hunter

Wandering off the beaten trail in search of game and new lands, these hardy adventurers naturally follow terrain and game trails to move more quickly than most. Their equipment is light but rugged enough to handle the thickest of thickets. Given time, supplies are at hand to be purchased from nature. Food, concoctions, and shelter, sooth the weary traveller for the morrow.

Off-road travel as if on-road for a short time period.

Battle Role:

Ranged Attacks/Protect party flank

  • Uses bow and arrows to maintain distance and deal high damage to a variety of targets.
  • Attacks: Ranged and single-handed Weapons.
  • Armor: Minor Metal Armor.
  • Has hit points that allow engaging in melee, but they don’t hold up well against a fighter.
  • Specialties: Hunting / Fishing, Scouting, Trapping, Concoctions, Off-Road Travel.
  • Focus Item: Hunter’s Kit – Outdoor gear and cooking supplies.

Primary attributes:

  • Steadiness

  • Acumen
