Rogue

Rogues are masters of Urban environments, stealth, and misdirection. Though considered to be worthless scoundrels by many, few are willing to challenge them on their own turf.

Rogues run the gamut from Robin Hood to outright thieves. Their skills at lockpicking, traps, disguise, and Flank attacks make them valuable companions and essential to any adventuring group. (Those same skills may warrant some consternation for personal belongings.) Though you may not be privy to all their methods, Rogues almost always prove their value in the long run.

Battle Role:

Flank and surprise

  • Uses thrown weapons and flank attacks to keep enemy off balance.
    Rogue flank attacks can deal significant damage.
  • Attacks: Thrown and short weapons
  • Armor: Clothing and Leather (no metal)
  • May engage in melee but shouldn’t stay long against more proficient opponents.
  • Specialties: Traps, Theft, Climbing/Balancing, Locks, Flanking, Disguise.
  • Focus Item: Rogues Kit – Trap parts, lock picks, make-up.

Primary attributes:

  • Agility

  • Acumen
