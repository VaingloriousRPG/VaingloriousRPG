Rogue

Rogues are masters of Urban environments, stealth, and misdirection. Though considered to be worthless scoundrels by many, few are willingness to challenge them on their own turf.

Battle Role:

Flank and surprise

  • Uses thrown weapons and flank attacks to keep enemy off balance. Rogue flank attacks can deal significant damage.
  • Attacks: Thrown and short weapons
  • Armor: Clothing and Leather (no metal)
  • May engage in melee but shouldn’t stay long against more proficient opponents.
  • Specialties: Traps, Theft, Climbing/Balancing, Locks, Flanking, Disguise.
  • Focus Item: Rogues Kit – Trap parts, lock picks, make-up.

Primary attributes:

  • Agility
