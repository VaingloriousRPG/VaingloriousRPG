Alquin

Battle Role:

Crystal Casters

  • Casts crystal based magic and has some force spells.
  • Attacks: Force spells and small hand weapons.
  • Armor: Clothing (no metal/shields/leather)
  • Avoids combat, has few hit points.
  • Specialties: Travel and caster support, Circles, Creations with other casters, Force, Scrying.
  • Focus Item:
  • May identify the presence of magic on an item and find what enhancements(aspects) it has.

Primary attributes:

  • Acumen
