Dokour

Battle Role:

Battle Casters

  • Stays back, throws damaging spells. Has no Nae'Em spells or partner class.
  • Attacks: Fire Spells and Small hand weapons.
  • Armor: Clothing and Leather (no metal/shields)
  • Avoids close combat, has few hit points.
  • Specialties: Fire and Heat, Darkness and Shadows, Dimension, Create Undead.
  • Focus Item:
  • Angry. Fire magic requires pain & angst to learn and utilize. Dokour with a high (good) alignment are routinely hunted by the majority of Dokour who are various shades of evil. Dokour even have spells designed to find good Dokour. No partner class.

Primary attributes:

  • Acumen
