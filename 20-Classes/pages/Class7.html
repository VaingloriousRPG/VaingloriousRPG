Dokour

Dokour play with fire, both magical and metaphorical. Dokour magic centers around spells of fire, dead (& undead), and the mysterious Dimension plane. Most all of their spells burn, even the begnin ones. Travelling to Dimension through a Dokour portal will singe a hardened adventurer, lesser souls will not survive the passing.

All this pain and power do not come without a cost. Dokour are most uniformly evil. They will hunt down their own kind who tread even the neutral path (dare not mention "good").

A caution against thinking they are of no use. A strong Dokour who swears to a goal can be worth their weight in gold. Cantankerous compatriots with a reasoning all their own.
