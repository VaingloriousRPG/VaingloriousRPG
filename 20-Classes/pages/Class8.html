Eolas

Battle Role:

Information Casters

  • Stays back, has some healing and some damaging spells. May make Nae'Ems with Rogue partners.
  • Attacks: Acid Spells and Small hand weapons.
  • Armor: Clothing and Leather (no metal/shields)
  • Avoids close combat, has few hit points.
  • Specialties: Hiding and revealing, Writing/Speaking languages, Oaths and Scrying.
  • Focus Item:
  • May identify the presence of magic on an item and find what enhancements(aspects) it has.
