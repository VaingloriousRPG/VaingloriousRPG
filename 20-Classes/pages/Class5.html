Strumos

Partners with fighters.

Battle Role:

Healer with some Electric Spells

  • Stays back and heals where needed. Has a greater range when healing Nae'Em connected Fighters.
  • Attacks: Electric Spells and Small hand weapons.
  • Armor: Clothing and Leather (no metal/shields)
  • Avoids combat, has few hit points.
  • Specialties:Electricity and shocks, Healing and reviving life, Stunning and buffing.
  • Focus Item:
  • May revive dead at 11th level. Healing concoctions are more effective when administered by a Strumos.

Primary attributes:

  • Acumen

  • Partners with Fighters
